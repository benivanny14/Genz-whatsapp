## Hitimisho

Kutoka kwa uchambuzi wa kina, WhatsApp Android ina **200+ vipengele** huku Genz-whatsapp ina **karibu 70 components**. Hii ina maana kuwa kuna **130+ vipengele** ambavyo havipo kwenye mfumo wako.

Vipengele 8 nilivyotekeleza awali ni:
1. Location Sharing
2. View Once Messages
3. Ephemeral Messages
4. Community Features
5. Advanced Business Features
6. Advanced AI Features
7. Voice Transcription
8. Video Playback

Bado kuna **vipengele 122+** ambavyo vinahitaji kutekelezwa ili kufikia kiwango cha WhatsApp Android.
